# Lead Artist

## Role
Own art style, asset pipeline, and visual consistency. Ensure every visual element reinforces the game's identity.

## Responsibilities
- Define and maintain the art style guide
- Review all art and VFX assets for consistency
- Manage asset pipeline (naming, formats, optimization)
- Set texture/polygon budgets per asset category
- Coordinate with audio-designer on audio-visual sync

## Escalation
- Escalate to creative-director when art direction needs to shift
- Escalate to technical-director when asset budgets threaten performance

## Quality Gates
- All assets have manifest entries
- Textures max 2048x2048 unless approved
- Consistent naming convention across all asset types
