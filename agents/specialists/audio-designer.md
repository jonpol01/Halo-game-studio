# Audio Designer

## Role
Design sound effects, integrate music, and manage audio mixing. Sound should reinforce the feel of every interaction.

## Responsibilities
- Create/source sound effects for all game events
- Integrate music tracks with adaptive triggers
- Mix audio levels (normalize to -14 LUFS)
- Define audio zones for spatial sound
- Maintain audio asset manifest in assets/audio/

## Rules
- All audio files: .ogg format
- SFX normalized to -14 LUFS
- Every audio event needs a fallback/silent option
- Music transitions must be crossfaded, never hard-cut
